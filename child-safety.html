NūrRing Child Safety Standards (CSAE)

This page sets out the child safety standards for NūrRing (the “App”). It is intended to comply with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards policy and relevant child protection laws.

1. Zero tolerance for CSAE and CSAM

NūrRing has a zero‑tolerance policy towards Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any content or behaviour that sexually exploits, abuses, endangers, or targets children is strictly prohibited in the App.

2. Prohibited content and behaviour

Users must not use NūrRing to engage in, promote, or facilitate:

  • Child sexual abuse or exploitation in any form.
  • Creation, possession, distribution, or sharing of CSAM.
  • Requests for explicit or sexual content involving children.
  • Grooming, solicitation, harassment, or threats involving minors.
  • Any behaviour that endangers the safety, privacy, or wellbeing of children.

NūrRing is a dhikr and wellbeing app, not a dating or explicit‑content platform. Any attempt to use the app for CSAE‑related behaviour is a serious violation of these standards and of our Terms & Conditions.

3. Reporting concerns

NūrRing provides ways for users to contact support and raise concerns about content or behaviour in the App. If you believe any content or activity may relate to CSAE or CSAM, please report it immediately via in‑app support options or by email.

Child safety contact: support@nurring.app

4. Handling and reporting CSAM

If we become aware of suspected or confirmed CSAM or CSAE activity in connection with NūrRing, we may remove the content, suspend or terminate relevant accounts, and report the matter to the appropriate authorities in line with applicable law. We will cooperate with lawful investigations and requests from competent authorities relating to child safety.

5. Compliance

NūrRing is operated with the intention to comply with applicable child safety and child protection laws, as well as Google Play policies on child safety and CSAE.

6. Updates

We may update these Child Safety Standards from time to time to reflect changes in law, best practice, or platform policy. The latest version will always be available on this page.
